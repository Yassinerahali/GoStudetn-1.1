Everything you need

Built for student life

Every feature designed around the real needs of Moroccan university students.

Student-verified accounts

All users are verified university students. Secure JWT authentication with role-based access for students and drivers.

Instant trip booking

Browse available trips, filter by city and date, reserve a seat and get an instant confirmation — all in seconds.

Digital receipts

Automatically generated PDF receipts for every booking with route details, driver info, price, and loyalty points earned.

Go Fidélité rewards

Earn 1 point for every 50 DH spent. Redeem for free snacks, cafeteria meals, or entire trips. Your loyalty pays off.

Live route map

Morocco-focused route visualization with animated paths, city markers, distance and duration estimates on every trip.

Pink Option

A dedicated safety mode that filters trips to female drivers only — creating a more comfortable environment for female students.

How GoStudent works

From sign-up to arrival, the whole process is built to be effortless.

1

Create your account

Sign up as a student or driver. Verify your university status and set up your profile.

2

Find or post a trip

Search available rides by route and date, or publish your own trip as a driver.

3

Reserve your seat

Book in one tap. Your seat is instantly confirmed and your receipt is generated.

Every trip earns GoPoints. Redeem them for snacks, meals, or your next free ride.
